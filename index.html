Nourish from within

Eating for who you are this week

Your body is not the same every day of the month — and your food shouldn't be either. Hormonal nutrition isn't a diet. It's a conversation with your body. Learning to give yourself what you actually need, when you need it.

Menstrual

Days 1–5

Rest. Restore. Iron-rich foods, warm meals, dark chocolate with intention. This is not the time to push — it's the time to replenish.

Follicular

Days 6–13

Energy rising. Lighter, fresher foods. Fermented foods, leafy greens, seeds. Start new things. Your mind is sharp.

Ovulatory

Days 14–17

Peak energy. Social, radiant, magnetic. Raw foods, fiber, and anti-inflammatory choices support this phase beautifully.

Luteal

Days 18–28

Slow down before you have to. Complex carbs, magnesium-rich foods, warmth. Honor this phase instead of fighting it.
